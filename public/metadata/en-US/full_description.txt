## About

PWA Google Tag Manager Pixel is an extension of the [Google Tag Manager App](https://github.com/vtex-apps/google-tag-manager) for tracking the activities PWA related of visitors to your ecommerce.
To see all the releases, [access the changelog](https://github.com/vtex-apps/pwa-gtm-pixel/blob/master/CHANGELOG.md)

## Features 

- Integration of PWA Events on your VTEX store with Google Analytics.
